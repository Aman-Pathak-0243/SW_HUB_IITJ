# Participant Sheet — Key & Instructions

This is the roster of test participants for the simulations. The machine-readable version
you import is **[participants-50.csv](participants-50.csv)**; the same 50 accounts are listed
below for easy reading and for handing out passwords.

## The columns

| Column | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **email** | The account's login (also its unique id). Uses the institute pattern `sim.user.NN@iitjammu.ac.in`. |
| **password** | The **initial** password. ⚠ The user is required to set their own new password on first login. |
| **name** | Display name shown on rosters, leaderboards and profiles. |
| **role** | Optional. Blank ⇒ a plain member. Valid keys: `normal_user`, `staff`, `coordinator`, `co_coordinator`, `secretary`. |

**Role mix in this sheet:** 45 × `normal_user`, 3 × `staff` (46–48), 2 × `coordinator` (49–50)
— enough to test how *normal users* and *staff* participate side by side.

**Password policy** (if you edit the sheet): ≥ 10 characters, with at least one uppercase,
one lowercase, and one digit. All provided passwords already comply.

## How to hand out passwords

After you bulk-import (see [../00-admin-setup/ADMIN_LOGIN_AND_SETUP.md](../00-admin-setup/ADMIN_LOGIN_AND_SETUP.md)),
send each participant their **email + initial password** and this note:

> _You have an account on the Student Affairs portal. Go to **/login**, sign in with the
> email and initial password below, and you'll be asked to **set your own new password**.
> Then open the event page and click **Register**._
> _Login: `sim.user.NN@iitjammu.ac.in` · Initial password: `Welcome#20NN`_
